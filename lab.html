Laboratory Information System

The Laboratory Information System manages laboratory operations, incoming requests from departments, data collection, and validation by the supervising physician. It sends laboratory test results via HAMSA to requesting departments or doctors. The system streamlines workflow, reporting, and ensures professional lab management for:

  • Efficient management of patient samples.
  • Alerts for abnormal laboratory results.
  • Display of current and previous results for each patient.
  • Quality control.
  • Automatic archiving of lab reports in the electronic patient record.
  • Integration with the queue management system for fast patient tracking.
  • Notification to staff via "music tone" for new tests—important for busy lab staff.
  • Display of room number in test requests for easy access to inpatient rooms for sample collection.

The system provides doctors with secure, easy access to results, reducing patient wait times. It supports a wide range of predefined laboratory tests (biochemistry, cytology, hematology, microbiology, serology, etc.).

Features

Workflow Management

  • Dedicated modules for daily lab operations and multiple sample types.
  • Integration with other systems for infection control, patient reports, and sample management.
  • Allow lab staff to enter results, query, prepare labels, and track sample storage.
  • Quality monitoring for all lab processes and results.

Sample Collection & Verification

  • Easy sample collection and tracking across labs.
  • Barcode label generation for samples.
  • System alerts for non-standard results.
  • Unlimited users and efficient sample processing (batch or individual).
  • Ability to regroup samples and reprint labels.
  • Direct tracking of delayed or pending samples.

Results Management

  • Enter results via system terminals.
  • Automated verification to speed up workflow.
  • Easy access to previous results for each patient.
  • Quick review and annotation of results by doctors.
  • Report writing, corrections, and integration with the advisory center for visual alerts.

Advanced Reporting & Quality Control

  • Results delivery based on user-defined criteria.
  • Access results via the Patient Data Center.
  • Easy printing and emailing of lab reports.
  • Define new quality control standards and results.
  • Quality control for samples and compliance with global standards.

Global Laboratory Test Library

The system is equipped with a global library of common laboratory tests, categorized by standard international nomenclature. You can add new groups and tests easily.

After defining and categorizing tests, they are moved to the available tests list for the lab. Tools allow adding or removing tests as needed.

Managing Available Lab Tests

  • Easy search for any test by entering part of its name.
  • Includes a global library of over 100 measurement units.
  • Mark tests as "Not Available" if equipment is down or supplies are out, hiding them from doctors and requesters.
  • Test pricing and grouping into packages for quick selection.
  • Create unlimited packages and add/remove tests as needed.
  • Enter standard criteria for each test (gender, age, range, abnormal low/high, etc.).

Sample Collection

Samples are collected by lab assistants (blood, urine, saliva, stool, etc.) and labeled with barcodes. The system tracks payment status and can accept or reject unpaid samples based on policy.

Lab Test Processing

Lab services match samples with system orders using barcodes, sorting samples for each patient for testing.

Entering Test Results

  • Manual entry: Lab staff enter results from device screens or visual readings, which are then evaluated and archived with the doctor's e-signature.
  • Archived results: When repeating tests, previous values are shown for comparison.
  • Device output archiving: The system archives printed device outputs in the patient record, even if the device can't export data directly.
