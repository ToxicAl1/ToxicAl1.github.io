Outpatient Manager

The Outpatient Management module (for non-admitted patients) is an innovative system designed to streamline the monitoring of a patientâ€™s condition during visits to outpatient or specialist clinics within hospitals and health centers. It documents the patient's condition and treatment at each visit and integrates seamlessly with other subsystems such as pharmacy, laboratory, radiology, administration, and billing for medical services.

This module helps organize outpatient clinic visits, reducing the burden of unscheduled patient reviews. The system offers the following key functionalities:

  • Appointment scheduling and management
  • Management of specialist outpatient clinics
  • Direct access to the Patient Information Center for monitoring and reviewing patient status
  • Organizing the required procedures for each patient
  • Sending requests such as: referrals, re-visits, radiology exams, summary reports, and more
  • Access to historical patient data through recorded medical consultations
