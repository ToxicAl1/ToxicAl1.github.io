Billing & Insurance

3.4. Billing
The payment system allows users to create a dedicated account statement for each patient, with new prices or by relying on predefined prices in the system, according to each patient and the services provided. The system also enables monitoring of payments from each department before executing assigned orders.

Integrated Insurance Solution

  • Initial definition of insurance companies.
  • Defining the patient's card and the amount paid by the patient.
  • Dispensing medications for insured patients.
  • Pricing medical services for each insurance company.
  • Reconciliation with the insurance company.
  • Accounts for participants in medical services for insured patients.
  • Partial payments not covered by insurance.
  • Archiving all insurance documents within the patient's file.
