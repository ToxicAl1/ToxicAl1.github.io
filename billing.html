Billing & Insurance

The HAMSA billing and insurance modules provide a unified solution for managing all patient financial transactions and insurance processes. Create detailed account statements for each patient, apply custom or predefined pricing, and monitor payments across departments. The system streamlines insurance workflows, from defining companies and patient cards to reconciliation and archiving, ensuring accuracy and compliance for every transaction.

How It Works

Billing

  • Create a dedicated account statement for each patient.
  • Apply new or predefined prices for services and items.
  • Monitor payments from each department before executing orders.
  • Print detailed receipts for all transactions.
  • Track partial payments and outstanding balances.

Insurance

  • Define insurance companies and patient insurance cards.
  • Set up pricing for medical services per insurance company.
  • Dispense medications for insured patients.
  • Reconcile and match with insurance companies.
  • Manage partial payments not covered by insurance.
  • Archive all insurance documents within the patient file.
