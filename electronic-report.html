Electronic Medical Reports

After completing all necessary examinations and registering medications for the patient, the system prints the electronic prescription, which includes:

Electronic Prescription Content

Patient Info & Barcode

Patient information and barcode for patient ID.

Diagnosis

Diagnosis according to ICD10 global standard.

Treatment

  • Continuation of previous treatment
  • New treatment
  • Discontinued treatment

Patient Status

Current patient status.

Next Visit Date & Reason

Date and reason for the next visit.

Doctor Info & E-signature

Doctor's information with electronic signature.

Medical Reports

Medical reports are essential for consultations, leaves, referrals, or any other medical reason.

Report Templates

The system provides ready-to-use templates for doctors to quickly prepare reports as needed.

Medical Report

Using templates or custom text, the system helps organize and number medical reports, which are archived in the patient's electronic record and signed electronically.

Doctor Data & E-signature

The system records doctor data, leave dates, and patient counts to help with scheduling. HAMSA supports electronic signatures for doctors, reducing paperwork and enabling digital hospital management.
